Frequently Asked Questions

Smart Garden is a web app that helps you take care of your plants by providing real-time information about their health and growth. By connecting sensors to your plants, Smart Garden can monitor factors such as temperature, humidity, and light levels, and give you personalized recommendations for watering, fertilizing, and more.

Yes, you can control your Smart Garden remotely using the Smart Garden mobile app. You can adjust settings such as watering schedule, lighting, and temperature, and monitor your plants' health from anywhere.

Yes, you will need to purchase Smart Garden sensors and water pumps in order to use the service. The sensors are used to monitor your plants' health, while the water pumps are used to automatically water your plants according to your customized schedule. You can purchase these items directly from the Smart Garden website or from a variety of authorized retailers.

Smart Garden can support a wide range of plants, including flowers, vegetables, herbs, and indoor plants. You can choose from a variety of pre-set plant profiles or create your own custom profile for a specific plant.
